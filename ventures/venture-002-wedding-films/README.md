# Venture 002 — Wedding Films

**State:** Validation
**Type:** Learning venture (skill-first)
**Revenue Target:** ₹50,000

## Objective
Become capable of producing cinematic wedding edits at a Bollywood / Hollywood storytelling level — and validate whether this is the fastest path to high-end editing skill and income.

## Why this exists
Not a decision to build a wedding company. A deliberate bet that mastering cinematic wedding editing is the **fastest path toward income and high-end editing skill**. The editing craft compounds back into Hospitality Media (Venture 001) and every future visual venture.

## The evidence bar (how this graduates)
This venture advances only on proof, not excitement:
- **Validation → Prototype:** one finished cinematic edit good enough to show a stranger.
- **Prototype → Execution:** a real inquiry or paid opportunity that the edit unlocked.
- **Execution → Revenue:** first cleared payment or advance.
