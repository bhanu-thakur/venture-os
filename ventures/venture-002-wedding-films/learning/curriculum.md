# Editing Curriculum — Venture 002 (Wedding Films)

A skill ladder, not a course. Each rung ends in a **finished export** — the only proof that the skill moved. Spend ~3–5 days per rung; advance when you've shipped the deliverable, not when you "understand" it.

## Rung 1 — The cut (rhythm & story)
- Learn the grammar: establishing → detail → emotion → payoff.
- Practice: rebuild a reference film's opening 20–30s (see `references.md`).
- **Deliverable:** one reconstructed sequence that matches the reference's pacing.

## Rung 2 — Music & sync
- Cut to the beat; let music drive emotion, not just play under it.
- Learn J-cuts / L-cuts so audio leads the eye.
- **Deliverable:** a 45–60s piece where every major cut lands on a musical moment.

## Rung 3 — Colour (the cinematic look)
- DaVinci colour page: balance → contrast → a deliberate look (teal-orange, warm filmic, etc.).
- Build 2–3 reusable LUT/power-grade looks you can apply fast.
- **Deliverable:** the Rung 2 piece, graded, plus a saved look you can reuse.

## Rung 4 — Story across a full film
- Structure a 2–3 minute highlight: arc, tension, release, callback.
- **Deliverable:** one full highlight edit from real or staged footage.

## Rung 5 — Signature & speed
- Develop a recognisable style; build templates/presets so a film takes hours, not days.
- **Deliverable:** a portfolio-grade highlight + a documented edit workflow (promote to `library/` once proven twice).

## Rule
Never skip the export. A rung is not complete until something is finished and shown to one real person.
