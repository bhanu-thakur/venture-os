# Practice Projects & Challenges — Venture 002 (Wedding Films)

Reps build the skill. Each challenge is small, finished, and shown to one person. Log every export — the streak of finished cuts *is* the portfolio forming.

## Editing challenges (do in order)
1. **The 30-second rebuild** — reconstruct a reference opening (matches `curriculum.md` Rung 1).
2. **Beat-cut drill** — 45s where every cut lands on the music.
3. **One-look grade** — take a flat clip and give it a deliberate cinematic look.
4. **The detail story** — 30s built only from close-ups (hands, rings, fabric, light).
5. **Emotion in 60s** — a mini-arc that makes one viewer feel something.

## Practice footage sources (no client needed)
- Your own iPhone 15 footage (stage a small shoot: a friend, a ceremony detail, candles).
- Free stock (Pexels, Mixkit) for wedding b-roll to practise cutting and grading.
- Re-edit existing footage you already have from Hospitality shoots.
