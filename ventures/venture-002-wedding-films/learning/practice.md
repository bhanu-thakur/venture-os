# Practice Projects & Challenges — Venture 002 (Wedding Films)

Reps build the skill. Each challenge is small, finished, and shown to one person. Log every export — the streak of finished cuts *is* the portfolio forming. The week's master plan and day-by-day schedule live in `/NEXT.md`; this file is the log and the standing list of backup reps for whenever you're blocked on someone else's reply.

## This week's core challenges (mirrors `curriculum.md` and `shooting.md`)

2. **Composition + movement session** (Shooting Rung 2–3) — Day 2.

## Practice footage sources (no client needed)
- Your own iPhone 15 footage (stage a small shoot: a friend, a ceremony detail, candles).
- Free stock (Pexels, Mixkit) for wedding b-roll to practise cutting and grading.
- Re-edit existing footage you already have from Hospitality shoots.

## While-you-wait bench (use these whenever blocked on a reply, daylight, or a render — see `NEXT.md`)
- Re-cut an already-shipped reel with a different opening 3 seconds; keep whichever wins.
- Study one unlogged reference film from `references.md` — full breakdown, not a passive watch.
- Shoot 10 minutes of unplanned b-roll wherever you are right now.
- Re-grade a clip with a second look and save it as a new power-grade option.
- Tighten your standard shot list with whatever Shooting Rung 5 taught you was missing.
