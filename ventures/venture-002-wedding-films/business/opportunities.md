# Opportunities & Leads — Venture 002 (Wedding Films)

Skill comes first; income follows proof. This file holds the **path to paid** so the learning stays pointed at a real outcome — but no cold outreach until one finished cinematic edit exists.

## The path to first income
1. **Build one proof edit** (see `mission.md` / `learning/`). Nothing sells without it.
2. **Start where trust already exists:** friends/family weddings, a junior/second-shooter slot for an established wedding filmmaker (learn on real shoots, get footage, get paid a little).
3. **Offer a free/low-cost teaser** for one upcoming wedding to break the portfolio deadlock (same wedge logic as Venture 001's free signature reel).
4. **Productise** once proven: teaser → highlight film → full film, with clear ROI framing.

## Where opportunities come from (India reality)
- **Second-shooter / editor-for-hire** for existing wedding studios — fastest way to real reps + income.
- **Referrals** from your own network (weddings are relationship-driven and seasonal — relationships matter more than ads).
- **Instagram** as the portfolio, not a cold funnel — post every finished cut.

## Lead capture
Use the workspace **Pipeline** to track real inquiries (couple / studio · next action · stage). Keep practice and leads separate — practice is in `learning/`, paid conversations are here.

## Assumptions to validate
1. Cinematic wedding editing is genuinely a faster income path than Hospitality Media — test with one finished edit + one real conversation before committing the month.
2. Second-shooter/editor roles are reachable in the Shimla / nearby belt (or remote editing for studios elsewhere).
