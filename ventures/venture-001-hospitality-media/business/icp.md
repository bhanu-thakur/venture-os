# Ideal Customer Profile — Venture 001

## Who (primary)
Owner-operated or manager-led **boutique and mid-range hotels** in Shimla and nearby hill-station belts (Mashobra, Kufri, Naldehra, Chail). Ideally properties with an in-house **restaurant or café** (the food angle) and a vibe/experience they sell on.

## Why they're a fit
- Decision-maker is **directly reachable** (owner/manager) — essential for a relationship-first, trust-deficit market.
- They compete on **ambience and experience**, which video conveys far better than their current phone photos.
- Their Instagram / OTA listings are typically **weak** — an obvious, visible gap.

## Pains
- Poor-quality social media and listing visuals; can't convey food or atmosphere.
- Reels visibly drive hill-station tourism, and they're not keeping up.
- No affordable, reliable local creator who "gets" hospitality.

## Where to find them
Instagram (search location + weak-but-active hotel/café accounts), Google Maps / OTA listings (MakeMyTrip, Booking) with dated photos, and in-person around Mall Road / Mashobra. Build a **target list of 10–15** to start.

## Disqualifiers
- Large chains / gatekept GMs (slow, relationship hard to start).
- Properties unwilling to grant shoot access.
- No interest in social presence at all.

## Assumption to validate
That food-led, owner-accessible boutique properties are abundant enough in the Shimla belt to fill a 10–15 prospect list. Confirm while building the list.
