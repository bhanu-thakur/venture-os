# Offer — Venture 001 (Hospitality Media)

**Positioning (assumption to validate):** A *premium, food-led* hospitality media specialist for boutique & mid-range hotels in the Shimla region — selling **more bookings and a stronger Instagram presence**, not "videography." Food cinematography is the spearhead skill; ambience/property reels extend from it.

## The wedge (breaks the trust + portfolio deadlock)
Offer **one free "signature reel"** to a target hotel — their best dish or their most photogenic corner, shot and graded for free. This is the Desi-market move: value first, zero upfront risk for them, relationship before transaction. It simultaneously builds the portfolio and earns trust. Cap this at **1–2 hotels** so "free" stays a wedge, not a habit.

## Productized ladder (after the wedge)
1. **Starter shoot** — half-day on-site → 3–5 finished vertical reels.
2. **Signature package** — full-day → 8–10 reels + a set of stills.
3. **Monthly content retainer** — a recurring batch of reels each month (the real prize: predictable revenue toward ₹50,000).

## Indicative pricing — HYPOTHESES, validate against 2–3 real quotes
> The Constitution requires evidence over opinion. Treat these as starting hypotheses to test in real conversations, priced for *paisa vasool* (clear ROI) yet premium for quality.
- Starter shoot: ~₹8,000–12,000
- Signature package: ~₹15,000–25,000
- Retainer: ~₹15,000–20,000 / month
Path to ₹50k: e.g., 2–3 signature packages, or one retainer over ~3 months.

## Desi-market terms
- **Minimal upfront:** no large advance cold. After they've seen the free spec reel (proof in hand), take a modest advance (e.g., 30–50%) on the first paid shoot.
- **Relationship-first:** in-person and WhatsApp over cold digital funnels.
- **Frame in their language:** bookings, guest experience, OTA listing quality — not "content" or "deliverables."

## Assumptions to validate
1. Niche is **food-led** hospitality media (from the food-cinematography skill). If the founder prefers full-property focus, the offer shifts.
2. Boutique/mid-range owners are reachable directly (owner/manager decision-maker).
3. Pricing ranges above — confirm with real prospects.
