# Outreach — Venture 001

**Principle:** value-first, relationship-first (Desi-market). Lead with proof and a free gift, not a pitch. Channel priority: **in-person → WhatsApp → DM**. Phone/relationship beats cold funnels here.

## Sequence
1. **Have the proof reel ready** (see `mission.md`) — never reach out empty-handed.
2. **Shortlist 10–15 hotels** (see `icp.md`).
3. **Open with value:** show the spec reel and offer to shoot **one signature reel free** for their property.
4. **Convert:** once they've seen value, propose the Starter shoot or Signature package (`business/offer.md`).
5. **Follow up** twice, spaced a few days apart, then park politely.

## Message template (WhatsApp / DM) — DRAFT, make it personal
> Namaste [Name] — I make short reels for hotels & cafés here in the hills. I shot this for [example/their type of place]: [link]. I'd love to make **one reel of your signature dish, free**, so you can see how it looks for [Hotel]. No cost, no commitment — when would suit you?

## In-person opener — DRAFT
> "I shoot food & hospitality reels for properties around Shimla — here's a quick one [show phone]. I'd be happy to do one for your place free so you can see it on your Instagram. Could I shoot your signature dish this week?"

## Why this works (Desi filter)
- **Zero upfront risk** for them → overcomes payment resistance.
- **Proof in hand** → overcomes the trust deficit.
- **Relationship + local presence** → the way hill-station business actually happens.

## Assumptions to validate
1. WhatsApp/in-person outperforms cold DM for this segment (likely, but test).
2. Owners will grant access for a free shoot (the core bet of the wedge).
