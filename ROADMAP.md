# ROADMAP

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active
**Governs:** How Venture OS matures over time — the strategic evolution of the operating system itself.

> This is **not a feature backlog.** It describes how the *operating system* grows in capability, phase by phase. Tactical tasks live in `NEXT.md`; venture work lives in venture files. Every phase here must serve the North Star and reduce one or more of the Four Costs (Thinking, Learning, Building, Deciding).

---

## HOW TO READ THIS ROADMAP

* **Phases are evidence-gated gates, not deadlines.** A phase advances only when its **exit criteria** are met — the same discipline the Venture States lifecycle applies to ventures (`PRODUCT.md` §5).
* **One phase is "current" at a time**, though work from later phases may begin early when it is the highest-leverage action.
* **Every phase has measurable outcomes.** If a phase cannot be measured, it cannot be exited.
* **The roadmap measures the OS, not any single venture.** Venture 001 appears only as the instrument that proves a phase.

---

## PHASE 0 — FOUNDATION (Genesis)

**Objective:** Stand up a coherent, bootable Core OS that a stateless AI can reconstruct from files alone.

**Entry criteria:** Repository initialized; Constitution locked.

**Exit criteria:**
* Core OS documents exist and are internally consistent: `PRODUCT.md` (frozen), `ROADMAP.md`, `CHANGELOG.md`, `ASSUMPTIONS.md`, `LESSONS_LEARNED.md`.
* The boot sequence in `START_HERE.md` reconstructs full state with no contradictions.
* The active venture is classified by a Venture State (not "Active").

**Measurable outcomes:** Boot audit passes with zero unresolved inconsistencies; all Core Primitives have a single home and single responsibility.

**Costs reduced:** Thinking, Deciding — a clean foundation removes ambiguity from every future session.

## PHASE 1 — FIRST PROVING GROUND

**Objective:** Drive one venture (Venture 001, Hospitality Media) through the lifecycle to its first cleared revenue, proving the OS can take a venture from Idea to Revenue.

**Entry criteria:** Phase 0 complete; Venture 001 classified (Validation → Prototype).

**Exit criteria:**
* Venture 001 reaches the **Revenue** state — at minimum the first cleared payment/advance (Proof of Execution).
* The first lessons are written to `LESSONS_LEARNED.md` and the first reusable Asset is captured.

**Measurable outcomes:** ≥1 venture validated with real demand; ≥₹50,000 revenue target progressed against; ≥1 portfolio-quality Asset produced; time from idea → first customer action recorded as a baseline.

**Costs reduced:** Building, Deciding — the OS proves the loop end-to-end on a real venture under real (Shimla, price-sensitive) constraints.

## PHASE 2 — FIRST COMPOUNDING (Extraction)

**Objective:** Convert Venture 001's lived experience into reusable Knowledge, Assets, and the first Core Engine(s) — turning one-off effort into leverage.

**Entry criteria:** A process inside Venture 001 has worked at least **twice** (satisfies the Core Engines Extraction Rule).

**Exit criteria:**
* ≥1 Core Engine documented (e.g., a validation, outreach, or content engine).
* Knowledge and Assets organized so a future venture can reuse them without rediscovery.

**Measurable outcomes:** ≥1 reusable engine in place; a measurable reduction in projected setup time for the next venture versus the Phase 1 baseline.

**Costs reduced:** Learning, Building — knowledge stops evaporating and effort starts compounding.

## PHASE 3 — PORTFOLIO (Multi-Venture)

**Objective:** Start a second venture using existing Engines and Assets, proving the OS scales beyond a single venture without scaling founder effort proportionally.

**Entry criteria:** ≥1 Core Engine exists and has been reused once.

**Exit criteria:**
* A second venture reaches **Validation** primarily on the strength of existing Assets/Engines.
* The OS demonstrably holds multiple ventures at once while focus stays protected on one active venture.

**Measurable outcomes:** Venture #2 reaches Validation in less time / lower cost than Venture #1 (the compounding test); portfolio held without loss of focus or context.

**Costs reduced:** Building, Learning, Deciding — cross-venture reuse is demonstrated, not just designed.

## PHASE 4 — MATURITY (Self-Improving Venture Machine)

**Objective:** Operate Venture OS as a repeatable venture machine that reliably converts ideas into revenue and continuously improves itself.

**Entry criteria:** ≥2 ventures validated; ≥2 Core Engines in service.

**Exit criteria:** Ongoing — this is the steady state, not a finish line.

**Measurable outcomes:** A predictable, shrinking idea → revenue cycle time; a growing library of Knowledge, Assets, and Engines; Constitution amendments reviewed and integrated on cadence.

**Costs reduced:** All four — the OS now lowers the cost of every new venture by default.

## HOW THIS DOCUMENT EVOLVES

The roadmap is reviewed at every **phase boundary** and on the **90-day amendment cadence** (`CONSTITUTION_AMENDMENTS.md`). Phases may be re-scoped, split, or reordered when reality teaches us something the plan did not anticipate — but a phase is never marked complete without meeting its exit criteria. It never absorbs tactical tasks. Every structural change bumps the version and is logged in `CHANGELOG.md`, with rationale in `DECISION_LEDGER.md`.
